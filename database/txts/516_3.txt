Subject: Direct acquisition of vaccines - Legal Amazon Consortium

The Covid-19 pandemic is a global concern resulting in unprecedented challenges of
sanitary, humanitarian and economical orders. In the Amazon region, the situation has
considerably worsened over the last months.

This matter was reported previously in a recent letter addressed by the nine former
environment ministers of Brazil, where they are requesting urgent aid to Germany, France and
Norway. Deforestation and environmental fires are factors that contribute to aggravate
respiratory complications caused by the new Coronavirus.

The worsening situation in Manaus, capital of the state of Amazonas, touches and
mobilizes the Brazilian society. The rapid spread of Covid-19 reaches very sensitive areas of
the Amazon region.

Considering the critical scenario and the necessity of immediate action, we would like
to verify the possibility of acquisition of the Sputnik Y vaccine by the Legal Amazon
Consortium. The acquisition of the Covid-19 vaccine seeks the effectiveness of the solution
recommended by the scientific community. We need to increment the immunization offer
throughout Amazon states in order lo save lives and creat conditions to sustainable
economic development in the entire region.

negociação direta dos 9 (nove) estados da Amazônia com o Sinopharm. A vacina do
Laboratório Sinopharm é autorizada pela Agência Chinesa, então, conseguiríamos com
muita brevidade a liberação para importação pelos estados e aplicação dessa vacina na

população.

O objetivo é complementar o Plano Nacional de Vacinação, fazendo com
que haja maior agilidade na aplicação das vacinas, tendo em vista os indicadores

gravemente crescentes e preocupantes na Amazônia nas últimas semanas.
